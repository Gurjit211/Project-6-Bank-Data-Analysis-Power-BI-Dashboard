# Project-6-Bank-Data-Analysis-Power-BI-Dashboard

This project is a Power BI dashboard created to analyze bank customer data.
It focuses on customer demographics, account balances, regions, job types, and customer growth over time.

Overview

The objective of this dashboard is to understand customer behavior and financial distribution within a bank.
It provides insights into how customers are distributed by age, gender, region, and job type, along with balance trends and customer onboarding patterns.

What the Dashboard Shows

Account balance by age and gender

Number of customers by age group and gender

Customer distribution by region

Account balance distribution by region

Number of customers by job type

Balance comparison by gender

Trend of customers joining the bank over time

Key metrics such as maximum and minimum balance age and dominant customer gender

Key Insights

Customers around the age of 34 hold the highest account balances

Younger and middle-aged customers form the majority of the customer base

Male customers slightly outnumber female customers

England contributes the highest number of customers and total balance

White-collar job holders represent a large portion of bank customers

Customer onboarding increased steadily over time

Visuals Included

Line chart for balance by age and gender

Bar chart for number of customers by age group and gender

Donut charts for regional, job type, and gender distribution

Time series chart showing customer join trend

KPI cards highlighting key summary metrics
